Exercise 2: Pair Reflection
Instructions
Take a moment to reflect individually about what you’ve learned about resumes in
this worksheet. Then, share your reflection with a partner and brainstorm ideas for
how each of you can tackle the particular challenges you anticipate as you create
your resume.
Tasks
1. Respond to the reflection prompts individually in the worksheet. Consider
the skills that you’ve learned in class and the professional skills you’re
bringing with you into your new tech career.
2. Talk about your responses with your partner and then brainstorm some
steps you can take to tackle the challenges each of you anticipated. Add
these ideas to your individual worksheets.
